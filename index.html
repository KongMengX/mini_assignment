My Favorite Animal, Ducks

I really like ducks because I feel like they are a very versatile animal. Ducks can be ruthless savages that chase you into a corner, much like geese.

However, they can also be seen as one of the more cuter animals on the internet, along side with cats, and dogs. I personally find ducks to be very cute and are great animals to draw.


Want some fun facts about ducks?

  • Bread is actually not healthy for ducks because it is not natural for them, unlike seeds.
  • There are over 30 different types of ducks!
  • Most ducks live 5-10 years.
  • Ducks have a 360 degree vision, meaning they can see almost all around themselves.
